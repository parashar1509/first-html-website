Peter Mckinnon

Internationally acclaimed Photographer, Filmmaker, YouTube Creator and Entrepreneur based in Toronto, Canada.

Peter uses his platforms to inspire and help people pursue their passions behind the lens. In 2019 he was named Breakout YouTuber of the Year at the Shorty Awards and in 2020 received a Streamy for Cinematographer of the Year. Peter’s work can be found in a number of outlets but a notable personal achievement for this Canadian was having his work featured on two special collection coins issued by the Royal Canadian Mint.

Peter McKinnon Wiki/Biography

Peter McKinnon’s channel has accrued over 5.26 million subscribers and 414 million views on the platform. He has been featured in Magic Magazine and several other photography magazines. He is one of the most popular social media influencers in Toronto, Canada.
